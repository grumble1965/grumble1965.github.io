Amateur Radio

I hold an FCC amateur radio Technician license with station ID KJ6WUW and I'm located in grid DN18LE. I rarely do voice, but enjoy DX listening and packet (really - it's fun to route like it's 1985).

Why just a Tech license? Because I think the cool radio stuff these days starts at VHF and goes up. Tech gives me access to all that spectrum with a minimum of fuss and without massive antennas. That said, I like to monitor HF ACARS, local government, and SW.

Equipment: Icom IC-92AD, Kenwood TH-D72, and a handful of SDR dongles (AirSpy, FunCube, etc.).
